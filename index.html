MAKEUP ROUTINE: STEP BY STEP

✿ = makeup products used

★ = tips!!

STEP 1: BASE

✿ E.l.f. Primer

✿ Estee Lauder Foundation

✿ Too Cool for School Finish Setting Pact

✿ Too Cool for School Artistic Book (Contour, Blush, Highlighter)

✿ Makeup Sponge

After applying skincare to your face, apply the E.l.f. primer.

Then, apply the foundation evenly on your face. Afterwards, using a makeup sponge, dab it all over your face to spread it out.

In order to keep that foundation on all day, use a powder brush to set your face using the Finish Setting Pact from Too Cool for School.

★ Set your face mainly on the T-zone area of your face (forehead, nose, and chin) because that's where it tends to get oily first

STEP 2: EYE MAKEUP

✿ Manga Lashes

✿ Lash Glue

✿ Eyeliner

✿ Eyeshadow

✿ Eyeshadow brush

✿ Scissors

✿ Eyebrow Pencil

You first have to prepare your lashes.

Take the lashes close to your eye and roughly measure the length of your eye to the lash.

Cut the lash band according the measurement you made earlier.

★ Before applying the glue on the band, make the lash into a U-shape and twist it to make it rounder. It creates a better shape for your eye to sit on!

Apply the glue lightly onto the lash band.

Wait until it gets tacky (20-30 seconds).

Apply the lash on top of your actual lash line.

★ Curl your real lashes before putting the fake ones on to make it seem more natural!

Now eyeliner! There are several styles, like the image below shows.

Apply eyeshadow on the water lid using the thin eyehsadow brush to create more depth, making the eyes look bigger.

★ You can also create "aegyosal" (or the fat underneath the eyes that show when you smile) using a lighter shade of eyeshadow to make your eyes look bigger.

Using the eyebrow pencil, outline the tails of your eyebrows and blend the inner parts of them.

You can also apply highlighter on the inner corner of your eyes.

STEP 3: LIPS

✿ Lip Balm

✿ Lip Tint (base/lighter shade)

✿ Lip Tint (inner/darker shade)

✿ Lipliner (*optional)

Use lip balm first to make sure your lips aren't chapped.

★ Exfoliate your lips and moisture them before applying anything so that they doesn't look patchy or dry.

Apply the lighter shade lip tint all over ur lips.

Apply the darker shade lip tint on the inner parts of the lips.

Smack your lips together a few times to blend.

★ If you want your lips to look thicker, you can overline them by using a lip pencil.
